Introduction

Welcome, digital content creators, to the world of accessibility and universal design practices! This webpage is your guide to understanding and implementing strategies that make digital content accessible and inclusive for all users, including those with disabilities.

Digital accessibility is about ensuring that websites, mobile applications, and all digital tools are designed with every user in mind. Universal design extends this concept by aiming to create environments and products that are inherently accessible to as many people as possible, without the need for further adaptation.

Here, you'll find a wealth of information on the principles of accessible design, alongside physical, real world examples to help further your understanding. You'll also learn best practices and practical guidelines to enhance your digital projects. Whether you're a designer, developer, content creator, or someone passionate about creating/learning about an inclusive digital world, this resource is designed to help you make impactful changes. Let's embark on this journey together and work towards a more accessible and empowering digital future!
